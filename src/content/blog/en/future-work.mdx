While everyone debates whether AI will replace designers, something remarkable is happening behind the scenes at forward-thinking companies around the world. But it's not what you'd expect.

This book outlines the shift in the creative process that is happening now, with realistic scenarios that will unfold in the workplace in the near future.

It's [available now on Apple Books](https://books.apple.com/us/book/future-work/id6754257455), and if you'd like to support me and my work by picking up a copy, it is greatly appreciated.
